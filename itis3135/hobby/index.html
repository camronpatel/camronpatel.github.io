When to hike?

The best times to hike depend on the season, location, and weather conditions, but usually, early morning and late afternoon are the best. Hiking in the morning, especially around sunrise, offers cooler temperatures, fewer crowds, and a higher chance of spotting animals. Late afternoon hikes can provide stunning sunset views but require careful planning to ensure you return before dark. Seasonally, spring and fall are often the best times for hiking, as temperatures are moderate, trails are less muddy than in winter, and the scenery is very vibrant by blooming flowers.

Getting started hiking is as easy as walking! First decide on a well planned and easy trail. Next pack comfortable walking shoes, snacks, water, a map, and a first aid kit. The more prepared you are the better! Lastly hike with a friend for safety and to have someone to enjoy the fun with!
